About This Dataset

This dataset contains patient health records from multiple clinics (Labcorp East, Labcorp West, BCBS Clinic) with comprehensive health metrics including age, BMI, blood pressure, diagnoses, treatments, lab results, and outcome scores.

Dataset: health_data_clinic.csv

Variables:

  • age: Patient age (continuous variable)
  • gender: Patient gender (categorical)
  • bmi: Body Mass Index (continuous variable)
  • systolic_bp: Systolic blood pressure (continuous variable)
  • diastolic_bp: Diastolic blood pressure (continuous variable)
  • diagnosis: Medical diagnosis (categorical: Healthy, Pre-Diabetes, Type 2 Diabetes, Hypertension)
  • treatment: Treatment prescribed (categorical)
  • lab_a1c: A1C lab test result (continuous variable)
  • hospital: Hospital/clinic name (categorical)
  • outcome_score: Treatment outcome score (continuous variable, 0-100)
