About This Dataset

This dataset contains 50 e-commerce sales records with detailed information about orders, including dates, categories, prices, quantities, customer segments, traffic sources, countries, and devices. This comprehensive dataset allows for multiple types of analysis.

Dataset: ecommerce_50_records.csv

Variables:

  • order_date: Date of the order (time series data)
  • category: Product category (categorical)
  • price: Product price (continuous)
  • quantity: Quantity purchased (continuous)
  • customer_segment: Prime or Regular (categorical)
  • traffic_source: Marketing channel (categorical)
  • country: Customer country (categorical)
  • device: Device type (Mobile, Desktop, Tablet)
  • revenue: Calculated as price √ó quantity
