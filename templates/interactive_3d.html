üé• 1) Matplotlib 3D

Capabilities: 3D scatter, 3D line, 3D surface plots

Advantages:

  • Simple 3D plotting
  • Most common classic method in Python

Disadvantages:

  • Not interactive (only slight rotation in notebooks)

Technology: Matplotlib (mpl_toolkits.mplot3d)

Best For: Static 3D visualizations

üî• 2) Plotly - Fully Interactive 3D (Most Powerful!)

Capabilities: Interactive 3D scatter, surface, line plots

Advantages:

  • Hover tooltips
  • Zoom and rotate
  • Click highlight
  • Can export as HTML
  • Excellent for real dashboards in healthcare, finance, e-commerce

Technology: Plotly (scatter_3d)

Best For: Interactive 3D with hover, zoom, and rotation - ‚≠ê BEST TECHNOLOGY for 3D + interactive

üåà 3) Bokeh - Interactive but Limited 3D

Technology: Bokeh
